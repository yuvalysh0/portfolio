## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### Colors
- **Light Mode**: Soft lavender with deep purple
- **Dark Mode**: Rich dark purple with neon pink
- **Gradients**: Purple to pink transitions
- **Contrast**: WCAG AAA compliant

### Animations
- **Purposeful**: Every animation has meaning
- **Smooth**: Custom easing, not robotic
- **Natural**: Spring physics
- **Performant**: GPU-accelerated
- **Accessible**: Respects reduced motion

### Interactions
- **Hover**: Scale 1.05-1.15x
- **Tap**: Scale 0.95x
- **Rotation**: 5-360° depending on element
- **Glow**: Radial gradient effects
- **Stagger**: 0.08-0.15s delays
